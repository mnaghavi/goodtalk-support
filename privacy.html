Privacy Policy

Plain-language privacy for GoodTalk.

GoodTalk is built to support sensitive conversations, so privacy and user choice matter. This page gives a simple overview of the kinds of information the app may use to run messaging, AI coaching, discovery, and support.

Quick summary

GoodTalk may use account details, message content, optional profile and discovery information, and support communications to operate the app. Some features rely on third-party infrastructure such as Firebase, Apple or Google sign-in, and server-side AI processing. GoodTalk does not sell your personal information.

Information GoodTalk may collect

  • Account and profile details such as your name, username, sign-in method, or avatar
  • Messages, drafts, and message metadata needed to deliver chats and power coaching features
  • Support emails, feedback, and bug reports you send to the team
  • Subscription or usage information needed for Premium and AI budget features
  • Optional phone, contacts, or location data only when those features are enabled by you

How information is used

  • To create and manage your account and conversations
  • To refine messages, generate coaching tips, and support @aria requests
  • To help people find each other through phone discovery, usernames, or invite flows
  • To send notifications, maintain security, and prevent abuse
  • To improve product quality, troubleshoot issues, and respond to support requests

AI and service providers

GoodTalk uses server-side AI processing for message refinement and assistant features. The app also relies on infrastructure and identity services to support authentication, real-time messaging, notifications, storage, and subscriptions. These providers may process information as needed to deliver the service on GoodTalk's behalf.

Sharing and sale of data

GoodTalk does not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with trusted vendors or service providers when that is necessary to run the app, support users, process subscriptions, maintain infrastructure, or comply with applicable legal obligations.

Your controls

  • You can choose whether to use features like phone discovery, contacts access, or location-aware assistant help
  • You can use a @username so people can find you without sharing a phone number
  • You can contact GoodTalk if you have questions about your account or privacy choices

Security and retention

GoodTalk uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the service, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Information may be retained for as long as needed to operate the app, resolve disputes, meet legal requirements, and support legitimate business and security needs.

Questions about privacy?

The fastest way to reach the team is by email. If your question is about support, troubleshooting, or an account issue, the support page may also help.
