Privacy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how GoodTalk collects, uses, stores, and discloses information in connection with the GoodTalk website, iPhone app, and related support and subscription services.

GoodTalk may also process account details, messages, profile settings, optional discovery data, and support communications to operate the service. Certain features rely on third-party services for authentication, real-time messaging, push notifications, subscriptions, and storage. AI features use OpenAI for server-side AI processing only after you allow AI data sharing in the app. GoodTalk is not an advertising-based service.

Information collected

Categories of information we may collect and use.

Account and profile

GoodTalk may collect and use information such as your name, username, avatar, linked sign-in providers, and account identifiers to create and maintain your account and profile.

Messages and message metadata

Messages, drafts, timestamps, visibility state, and related metadata may be processed to deliver chats, show message status, and support features such as refinement approval and conversation history.

Phone verification and discovery

If you verify a phone number or enable phone-based discovery, GoodTalk may process that information to support account verification and user discovery, subject to the settings applied by you and other users.

Contacts access

If you grant contacts access, GoodTalk may process contact information to help identify which people in your contacts are already using the service. The service is designed to support privacy-aware matching rather than exposing raw contact lists in the product interface.

Use of information

How information may be used to provide the service.

AI processing for Coach, Touch-up, and Aria

When you first try Coach, Touch-up, or @aria, GoodTalk asks for your permission before sending personal data to OpenAI for AI processing. If you allow AI data sharing, GoodTalk may send OpenAI the message draft or AI request you ask it to process, eligible existing and future conversation messages authored by users who have allowed AI data sharing, AI-generated conversation summaries derived only from eligible messages, display names and tone/profile details for users who have also allowed AI data sharing, selected AI settings such as model and measurement units, and optional location context when you approve location-based Aria help.

Your partner's message text, display name, tone profile, or any summary derived from that partner's messages is not sent to OpenAI unless that partner has also allowed AI data sharing. If one conversation participant allows AI data sharing and the other does not, GoodTalk sends only the allowing participant's own content and AI summaries scoped to allowing participants. Normal messaging remains available if you do not allow AI data sharing. GoodTalk does not sell AI data, use it for advertising, build marketing profiles from it, share it with data brokers, or opt in to have OpenAI train models on it.

Optional location for Aria

If you grant location access, GoodTalk may use location context to improve certain Aria responses, such as local or measurement-aware answers. Location access is optional and tied to that feature set.

Notifications and device tokens

If you enable notifications, GoodTalk may use push-notification permission status and device token information to deliver message alerts and related notification features.

Subscriptions and Premium

GoodTalk uses App Store subscription information to manage Premium access, restore purchases, and determine free versus Premium feature availability.

Service providers

Third-party providers that help operate GoodTalk.

Authentication providers

GoodTalk supports sign-in through phone, Apple, and Google, depending on the sign-in method selected by the user.

Messaging and storage infrastructure

The app uses cloud infrastructure for authentication, messaging, storage, real-time synchronization, subscriptions, and notification-related functions.

Server-side AI providers

AI-enabled features use OpenAI to process supported requests and return responses for refinement, coaching, and assistant functions after the user allows AI data sharing in the app.

App-level settings and preferences

GoodTalk stores local preferences such as selected defaults and presentation choices so the app can remember user settings and preferred behavior.

User controls

Settings and choices available to users.

Privacy and discovery controls

  • Enable or disable discoverability by phone
  • Use @username so people can find you without a phone number
  • Set last-seen visibility
  • Choose whether to grant contacts or location access

Product and account controls

  • Choose a default style for new chats
  • Adjust notification and sound preferences
  • Choose Aria measurement preferences
  • Allow, revoke, or re-enable AI data sharing for OpenAI-powered features
  • Manage Premium through the App Store

Legal details

Full policy and legal disclosures.

Last updated: May 6, 2026. This section contains the fuller policy language, legal disclosures, and contact paths related to the collection, use, disclosure, retention, and deletion of information.

Operator and scope

GoodTalk is operated by Aphelion Software in Ontario, Canada. Aphelion Software is responsible for personal information under its control and for this Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy applies to the GoodTalk website, the GoodTalk iPhone app, and related support and Premium flows that link to this page.

Questions, complaints, access requests, correction requests, deletion requests, and other privacy inquiries can be sent to contact@good-talk.app.

Categories of data

Depending on how you use GoodTalk, the app may process account identifiers, profile information, message content, draft content, timestamps, presence and visibility settings, phone verification data, discoverability settings, optional contact-matching inputs, optional location context for Aria, notification tokens, subscription status, AI data-sharing consent status, and support communications.

Why the data is used

GoodTalk uses this information to authenticate users, deliver chats, power message refinement and coaching features, return Aria responses, manage subscriptions, protect accounts, support discovery and connection features, respond to support requests, enforce product rules, and improve reliability and safety.

Providers and processing partners

GoodTalk relies on service providers to operate the app. Based on the current app stack, those providers may include Apple for App Store billing, Sign in with Apple, and platform services; Google for Sign in with Google; Firebase and Google Cloud for authentication, messaging, storage, notifications, and server-side functions; and OpenAI for AI-assisted processing used by Coach, Touch-up, and Aria after the requesting user allows AI data sharing.

GoodTalk shares personal information with service providers only where reasonably necessary to operate, secure, support, or improve the service. GoodTalk requires processors handling personal information on its behalf to provide safeguards, confidentiality protections, and handling standards that are consistent with this Privacy Policy and applicable law.

AI output and user review

AI-assisted features may produce output that is inaccurate, incomplete, incorrect, or offensive. Output can sound confident even when it is wrong. You should review AI-assisted output before relying on it or sharing it, and you should not treat it as a sole source of factual truth or as a substitute for professional advice, medical advice, legal advice, crisis support, or other expert guidance.

You can decline AI data sharing and continue using normal messaging. You can also revoke or re-enable AI data sharing from Settings. Revoking AI data sharing blocks future OpenAI processing tied to your account; it does not change your free or Premium subscription limits. If you decline or revoke AI data sharing, GoodTalk will not send your content to OpenAI or any third-party AI provider for AI features. If you want to request deletion of eligible data, you can delete your account in the app or contact contact@good-talk.app.

OpenAI processing details

For AI features, GoodTalk sends only the data needed for the requested AI task and only within the consent scope described above. GoodTalk uses OpenAI API services. OpenAI states that API data is not used to train or improve OpenAI models unless the API customer opts in, and that abuse-monitoring logs may be retained for up to 30 days unless a different retention control applies. GoodTalk does not sell AI data, use it for advertising, build marketing profiles from it, share it with data brokers, or opt in to have OpenAI train models on it.

Retention and deletion

GoodTalk keeps information for as long as it is reasonably needed to operate the service, maintain account history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the app. Retention can vary by data type. If you delete your account or request deletion, some information may be removed immediately while certain records may be retained longer where required for security, fraud prevention, billing, or legal compliance.

GoodTalk aims to delete or de-identify personal information when it is no longer required for the purposes for which it was collected, subject to technical limits, backup cycles, legal requirements, and legitimate security or fraud-prevention needs.

Your rights and requests

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access to personal information, correction of inaccurate information, deletion, withdrawal of consent where applicable, or additional information about how your personal data is handled. GoodTalk also provides in-app controls for phone discoverability, last-seen visibility, contacts access, location access, notifications, and account deletion.

Privacy requests can be sent to contact@good-talk.app. GoodTalk may need to verify identity before completing certain requests. Consent can also be withdrawn by discontinuing optional permissions or deleting your account, though some processing may continue where required to operate the service you still use or to comply with law.

Children and sensitive use

GoodTalk is not intended for children under 13, or under any higher minimum age required by local law. Because the product can involve personal or emotionally sensitive conversations, users should avoid sharing information they would not want processed to operate messaging, support, and AI-assisted features. AI-assisted features require in-app permission before personal data is sent to OpenAI.

International processing and updates

Service providers used to operate GoodTalk may process information in Canada, the United States, or other countries where they operate. Data protection rules can differ across jurisdictions. GoodTalk may update this Privacy Policy as the app changes, and the effective date at the top of this legal section will be updated when material revisions are made.
