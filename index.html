Three Core Modes

The app is built around three different kinds of help.

Some moments need coaching. Some just need polish. Others need a smart assistant in the room. GoodTalk separates those experiences so the support feels intentional, not generic.

Coach

For the conversations you do not want to get wrong.

Coach is the most supportive mode in GoodTalk. It helps reshape emotionally sharp drafts into messages that are more constructive, more grounded, and easier for the other person to hear.

  • Built for harder conversations where tone and emotional clarity matter
  • Includes review and approval before a refined message is sent
  • Can show a private coaching insight about why the message changed
  • Premium-only mode for deeper communication support
Aria

A capable assistant, right inside the chat.

Aria gives GoodTalk a more open-ended mode. Mention @aria to ask for help with wording, perspective, planning, or practical questions without breaking the flow of the conversation.

  • Use @aria directly inside a conversation
  • Helpful for advice, brainstorming, everyday questions, and perspective
  • Supports measurement preferences and optional location-aware context
  • Available on free with limits, with broader access on Premium
Touch-up

For the messages that only need a better finish.

Touch-up is the lighter, faster mode. It keeps your original meaning intact while cleaning up grammar, improving clarity, and softening the edges just enough to help your message land better.

  • Best for everyday texting, quick edits, and better readability
  • Designed to preserve your voice instead of rewriting the whole message
  • Useful when you want polish without a full coaching pass
  • Works naturally with the app's approval flow before a message is sent
  • Included on free with usage limits and expanded on Premium

Features

Everything is grouped around better communication, not clutter.

GoodTalk is more than one clever message tool. It is a communication product with discovery, personalization, privacy, and subscription choices built around how people actually talk.

What the product feels like

Messaging first, with AI support layered in where it helps.

The app stays rooted in real conversations. You see who is active, move between chats naturally, and only bring in more help when you want it.

Communication modes

  • Coach for deeper message refinement and private coaching insight
  • Touch-up for grammar, clarity, and softer phrasing
  • Aria for in-chat assistance through @aria
  • Message review and approval flows before sending

Discovery and connection

  • Phone sign-in plus Apple and Google account options
  • @username handles so people can find you without sharing a number
  • Phone discoverability when enabled
  • Invite-code pairing and contacts matching

Personalization and settings

  • Default style for new chats
  • Per-chat settings for mode, approval behavior, mute, and background
  • Notification and sound preferences
  • Aria unit preferences and AI model selection

Privacy and permissions

  • Optional contacts access for matching
  • Optional location context for Aria features
  • Last-seen privacy settings
  • Server-side AI handling and account-linked controls
