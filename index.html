What makes GoodTalk different

This is not just another messenger with AI bolted on. GoodTalk is built around the emotional reality of close conversations: drafts that come out too sharp, moments where you need help finding the right words, and the need to stay kind without sounding robotic.

AI-powered coaching

Coach Mode helps reshape charged messages into something more grounded, constructive, and easier to hear. It is designed around compassionate communication principles, so the app helps reduce escalation instead of adding to it.

Touch-ups that respect your voice

Not every message needs heavy coaching. Touch Up Mode improves wording, grammar, and clarity while keeping your original meaning intact, which makes GoodTalk useful for everyday chats too.

@aria in the flow

Mention @aria directly inside a conversation to ask for advice, perspective, brainstorming help, or context-aware nudges while things are unfolding.

Designed for real connection

  • Message review and approval before sending
  • Private coaching insights attached to refined messages
  • Premium features for deeper coaching and more AI usage
  • Accessibility-first approach with VoiceOver and Dynamic Type support

Easy ways to find the right person

  • Find someone by phone when enabled
  • Use a unique @username instead of sharing your number
  • Create short-lived invite codes when you want a cleaner handoff
  • See familiar names and handles inside the app, not just anonymous accounts

How it works in practice

GoodTalk keeps the workflow simple enough for real life: type naturally, ask for help when you want it, review the improved message, then send something closer to what you actually meant.

1

Write the honest draft

Start with the message you would normally send. GoodTalk is built around helping with real emotion, not forcing you into canned language from the start.

2

Refine, touch up, or ask Aria

Choose the level of support you need. Use a light touch-up, a fuller coaching pass, or bring @aria into the conversation when you want ideas, wording, or perspective in the moment.

3

Approve and send with more confidence

Review the final draft before it goes out. The result is a conversation that sounds more like your best self and less like your worst five seconds.

Trust matters here

Communication tools only work if people feel safe using them. The app is structured around permission-based features, server-side AI processing, and optional discovery paths that let people connect in the way that feels right for them.

Privacy-minded product choices

GoodTalk can use phone verification, contact matching, usernames, and optional location context for @aria, but those experiences depend on the permissions and settings a person chooses. Messaging, support, and AI features are backed by Firebase services, with AI requests handled server-side.

Built for support, not guesswork

Need help with sign-in, discovery, subscriptions, or feedback? The support page gives people a direct route to the team, and the privacy page explains the app in clear, plain language.
