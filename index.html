What GoodTalk does

GoodTalk helps you send messages that are clearer, more respectful, and more true to what you mean.

Core features

  • Coach Mode
    Refine your message before sending. GoodTalk helps reduce harsh wording, improve tone, and make conversations more thoughtful.
  • Touch Up Mode
    Keep your original meaning while improving grammar, clarity, and phrasing. It respects your voice instead of replacing it.
  • Aria Mode
    Ask @aria for help directly in your chat. Get suggestions, perspective, and support while the conversation is happening.
