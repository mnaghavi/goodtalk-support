Helpful context

A few details can make support much faster.

You do not need a formal bug report. These details just help the team understand what happened faster.

For bugs or broken flows

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes
  • Your device model and iOS version if relevant

For account or billing questions

  • Your sign-in method
  • Whether the issue is about free access, Premium, or restoring purchases
  • Any error message you saw
  • Whether the issue affects one device or more than one
Quick answers

Short answers to common support questions.

What is the difference between Coach and Touch-up?

Coach is the deeper, more guided mode for harder conversations. Touch-up is lighter and focused on grammar, clarity, and phrasing while staying close to your original message.

Do I need to share my phone number to connect with someone?

No. GoodTalk supports several connection paths, including phone-based discovery, @username, and contact matching.

Is Coach available on the free version?

Coach is part of the Premium experience. The free version still includes messaging plus limited access to Touch-up and Aria.

Where should I send feedback?

Feature requests, usability feedback, and bug reports can all be sent to support@good-talk.app.

Need the team?

Send your question directly.
